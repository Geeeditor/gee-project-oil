WHAT HAS BEEN SAID ABOUT US

Kurasha Petroleum is among one of the best Kazakhstan Republic trading petroleum company, a vertically integrated holding. Oil and gas production, oil refining, petrochemistry, a tire complex and a gas station network are stably developing as part of the Company's production complex. The Kurasha Petroleum also participates in the capital of the financial (banking and insurance) sector. Today, the Oil Refinery Complex is a full member of the oil refining industry in Kazakhstan Republic and produces highly competitive, environmentally friendly with all kinds of products including Euro-5 diesel fuel, aviation kerosene of the RT, TS-1 and Jet A-1, D6 brands, high-index base oils of Group II and III. With the completion of the project.

It is planned to produce a wide range of products of high oil processing. Already today, the Kurasha Petroleum occupies a worthy place among the largest participants in the oil refining industry in Kazakhstan Republic, shaping the market and is known far abroad. Kurasha Petroleum continues to invest in its fixed assets for the utilization of associated gas in accordance with its plan, which will allow the Company to achieve over 95% of gas utilization at all developed fields.

MORE ON THE COMPANY

LLP «AOK-OIL» is a uniquely transform premier infrastructures for functional petrochemicals production. Completely sustainable leadership for economically sound sources.

OOO LLP «AOK-OIL» is a subsidiary of the LLP «AOK-OIL» Group of Companies. The main field of activity of the enterprise: Search, intelligence service, drilling, development and operation of oil and gas fields. OOO LLP «AOK-OIL» holds 19 licenses for the right to use subsoil for geological exploration, exploration, production of hydrocarbons and develops 34 fields with high geological risks due to their small size, with difficult mining and geological conditions and high product viscosities. The geography of the Society's activities covers ten administrative regions of the Samara region, eight administrative regions of the Orenburg region and one administrative region of the Ulyanovsk region. LLP «AOK-OIL» manages 245 drilled wells and 12 oil loading points. In 2009, an oil treatment unit with an acceptance point and a system for measuring the quantity and quality indicators of oil was put into operation. In 2015, a gas treatment unit was commissioned, which made it possible to achieve the required indicators for APG utilization in a cost-effective way.
